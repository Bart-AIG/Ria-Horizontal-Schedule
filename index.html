Schedule Guide

Full Construction Schedule with Cost Loading for Ria at Round Rock horizontal construction (48 tasks, 6 phases). Tracks all activities from preconstruction through close-out and acceptance.

Cost data source: JL Gray Construction Exhibit B Bid Form dated 2/19/2026. Total base bid: $4,146,104.60.

Features:

  • Click any task row to see cost breakdown by bid line item, dates, predecessors, and notes.
  • Cost on Bars toggle shows dollar amounts directly on the Gantt bars.
  • Critical Path Only filters to critical and near-critical tasks.
  • Dependencies toggle draws predecessor arrows between tasks.
  • Float badges show days remaining to target on each active task.
  • Alert system flags overdue, at-risk, and blocked tasks.
  • Actual dates override the schedule. Enter actual start/end on any task and the cascade recalculates.
  • Lag/Lead on predecessor links. Negative = overlap (start before pred finishes). Positive = wait gap.
  • Base vs Projected dates in detail panel. Base dates are the original plan; projected dates are what the schedule runs on.
  • Root cause tracing in delay alerts shows what changed to cause the slip.
  • Cloud save via JSONBin.io. Click ⚙ to configure, then ☁ Save pushes to cloud.
  • Interest cost bar shows daily interest carry based on cumulative loan draws from Master Budget.
  • Phase headers show phase subtotals and collapse/expand with arrow toggles.
  • Export Notes generates a text summary with costs.

Note: Dry Utilities (IDs 21-25) are NOT IN CONTRACT and show $0. Cost mapping is approximate where bid items span multiple schedule activities.
